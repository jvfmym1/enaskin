## The Ingredient Breakdown

Let's talk science for a minute. EnaSkin's formulations aren't just throwing trendy ingredients into a bottle and hoping for the best.

**Niacinamide (Vitamin B3):** Reduces melanin transfer to skin cells, evening out tone and fading spots. Also strengthens the skin barrier and reduces inflammation.

**3-O-Ethyl Ascorbic Acid:** A stable form of vitamin C that brightens skin and boosts collagen without the irritation some people get from pure ascorbic acid.

**Alpha-Arbutin:** Inhibits tyrosinase, the enzyme responsible for melanin production. It's gentler than hydroquinone but still effective.

**4-Butylresorcinol:** A powerful brightening agent that's been shown in studies to be more effective than kojic acid at inhibiting melanin.

**Kojic Acid:** Another melanin production inhibitor, particularly effective on sun spots and age spots.

**Lactic Acid & Salicylic Acid:** Gentle exfoliants that remove dead skin cells, allowing newer, brighter skin to surface while improving product absorption.

**Retinol:** Accelerates cell turnover, fades pigmentation, and stimulates collagen production for anti-aging benefits.
